Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 22, 2025
Privacy Summary: GLP-1 Diary prioritizes your privacy. Your health data stays on your device and is integrated with Apple HealthKit. We do not collect, store, or share your personal health information.

1. Introduction

Metamorph ("we," "our," or "us") operates the GLP-1 Diary mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Health Data

The App accesses and stores the following health information locally on your device:

  • Weight Data: Body weight measurements through Apple HealthKit
  • Symptom Data: User-reported symptoms related to GLP-1 medication
  • Dates: Timestamps associated with health entries

2.2 Subscription Data

For premium subscriptions, Apple processes payment information through their secure payment system. We do not have access to your payment details.

2.3 Technical Data

We may collect minimal technical information for app functionality, including:

  • App crashes and performance data
  • Device type and iOS version (anonymized)
  • Subscription status

3. How We Use Your Information

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
Health Data App functionality, tracking, visualization Your device only
Subscription Data Premium feature access Apple's servers
Technical Data App improvement, bug fixes Anonymized analytics

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your health data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Apple HealthKit: Weight data with end-to-end encryption
  • Device Storage: Symptom data encrypted at rest
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your health data on external servers

4.2 Data Export

Premium users can export their data in CSV or text format. Exported files are:

  • Generated locally on your device
  • Shared through iOS standard sharing mechanisms
  • Not stored or transmitted through our servers

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal health information to third parties. Data may only be disclosed:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights or safety

6. Apple HealthKit Integration

Our App integrates with Apple HealthKit under these principles:

  • Data is stored in Apple's secure HealthKit framework
  • You control all permissions through iOS Settings
  • We cannot access HealthKit data without your explicit permission
  • You can revoke access at any time through iOS Settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data within the App
  • Delete: Remove data by uninstalling the App
  • Export: Download your data (Premium feature)
  • Control: Manage HealthKit permissions through iOS Settings

9. International Data Transfers

Since your health data is stored locally on your device and not transmitted to our servers, there are no international data transfers of your personal health information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending notifications for material changes
